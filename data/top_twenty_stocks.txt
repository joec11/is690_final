**List of the top 20 stocks**:
1. **Apple Inc. (AAPL)**: A technology giant known for its consumer electronics, software, and digital services, including the iPhone, iPad, Mac computers, and services like iCloud and Apple Music.
2. **Amazon.com Inc. (AMZN)**: A global e-commerce and cloud computing company known for its online retail platform, Amazon Prime subscription service, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and digital streaming services.
3. **Microsoft Corporation (MSFT)**: A multinational technology company known for its software products, including the Windows operating system, Office suite, and cloud services through Azure.
4. **Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL / GOOG)**: The parent company of Google, Alphabet is a technology conglomerate involved in online advertising (through Google Ads), cloud computing (Google Cloud), software (Android OS), and hardware (Pixel phones, Nest devices).
5. **Tesla Inc. (TSLA)**: A leading electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer and energy company, known for its electric cars, solar energy products, and battery storage solutions.
6. **Meta Platforms Inc. (META)**: Formerly Facebook, Meta Platforms focuses on social networking services through Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Oculus virtual reality products.
7. **Johnson & Johnson (JNJ)**: A global healthcare company involved in pharmaceuticals, medical devices, and consumer health products, including brands like Tylenol, Band-Aid, and Johnson's Baby products.
8. **Visa Inc. (V)**: A global payments technology company facilitating electronic funds transfers worldwide through its Visa-branded credit, debit, and prepaid cards.
9. **Walmart Inc. (WMT)**: A multinational retail corporation known for its chain of hypermarkets, discount department stores, and grocery stores, operating under the Walmart brand.
10. **Procter & Gamble Co. (PG)**: A multinational consumer goods corporation known for a wide range of products in segments like beauty, grooming, health care, fabric and home care, and baby, feminine, and family care.
11. **Adobe Inc. (ADBE)**: A multinational computer software company known for its creativity and multimedia software products, including Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat, and Adobe Creative Cloud.
12. **Salesforce.com Inc. (CRM)**: A cloud-based software company that provides customer relationship management (CRM) service and enterprise applications focused on sales, service, marketing automation, and analytics.
13. **PayPal Holdings Inc. (PYPL)**: A technology platform and digital payments company that enables digital and mobile payments on behalf of consumers and merchants worldwide through its PayPal and Venmo platforms.
14. **Netflix Inc. (NFLX)**: A global streaming entertainment service offering a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more across a diverse range of genres and languages.
15. **NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA)**: A leading technology company focused on graphics processing units (GPUs) for gaming and professional markets, as well as system-on-a-chip units (SoCs) for mobile computing and automotive markets.
16. **AMD (Advanced Micro Devices, Inc.) (AMD)**: A semiconductor company that develops computer processors and related technologies for business and consumer markets, competing with Intel in the CPU market and NVIDIA in the GPU market.
17. **Square, Inc. (SQ)**: A financial services and digital payments company known for its Square Point of Sale (POS) system, Cash App, and other financial services for individuals and businesses.
18. **Intel Corporation (INTC)**: A technology company primarily known for designing and manufacturing semiconductor chips and related products for computing and communications industries.
19. **Cisco Systems, Inc. (CSCO)**: A multinational technology conglomerate that designs, manufactures, and sells networking hardware, telecommunications equipment, and other high-technology services and products.
20. **The Walt Disney Company (DIS)**: A diversified global entertainment company known for its iconic film studios (Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Lucasfilm), theme parks, media networks (ABC, ESPN), and consumer products.
